About Exo-Craft

Freshly open and welcoming mature, 16+ members! Exo-Craft is a place for creative minds to come together, build unique designs, and enjoy a peaceful community where everyone is a neighbor and a friend.
We are a semi-vanilla Minecraft server running on Fabric 1.21.5, enhanced with performance mods for smoother gameplay. No complex plugins, just polished Minecraft!

A Welcoming Community

We pride ourselves on being an anti-grief, LGBT+, open, welcoming, and friendly community for mature (16+) players. Join us for a peaceful experience focused on creativity, collaboration, building, exploration, and adventure.

Enhanced Gameplay Features

Enjoy quality-of-life features like 1 Player Sleep. We also utilize unique tools like Bot Spawning (via Carpet Mod) for optimizing farms without needing alt accounts. Explore the possibilities on our Fabric 1.21.5 server!

Transparency & Performance

We believe in transparency with a public (read-only) server console and a single shared operator account for accountability. The world is pregenerated to 10k blocks, and the server runs on robust hardware (Ryzen 9950x, 8GB DDR5 RAM, NVME Storage) with DDoS protection for maximum uptime.

Events & Our Story

Look forward to adventure-packed events during holidays and throughout the year! We formed after leaving a toxic, pay-to-win server environment. Our goal is to foster a community built on the love for Minecraft's core elements. Learn more about our journey on Discord!
