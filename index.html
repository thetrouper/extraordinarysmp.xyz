About Extraordinary SMP

Freshly open and welcoming mature, 16+ members! Extraordinary SMP is a place for creative minds to come together, build unique designs, and enjoy a peaceful community where everyone is a neighbor and a friend.

We are a semi-vanilla Minecraft serverus running on Fabric 1.21.5, enhanced with performance mods for smoother gameplay. No complex plugins, just polished Minecraft!

We pride ourselves on being an anti-grief, LGBT+, open, welcoming, and friendly community.

Key Features & Mods

1 Player Sleep

No more waiting for everyone to sleep! Just one player sleeping is enough to pass the night.

Bot Spawning Mod (Carpet Mod Feature)

This unique feature allows you to spawn bots using the command /player [username] spawn at ~ ~ ~. This is incredibly useful for multi-dimensional farms, eliminating the need for alt accounts.

You can control these bots to move, attack, or drop items they might pick up. Feel free to experiment and see how they can optimize your builds!

Events & Future Plans

Get ready for adventure-packed events during holidays and throughout the year! We're planning creative challenges, surprises, and more to keep you engaged and entertained while enjoying the peacefulness of the SMP.

Transparency

We believe in transparency. The server console is public (read-only), allowing anyone to audit administrative actions. We have nothing to hide, unlike others.

There is only one operator account, shared by all admins, ensuring accountability.

World Pregeneration

The world has been pregenerated out to 10k blocks from spawn. This significantly reduces lag when traveling relatively short distances, providing a smoother exploration experience.
